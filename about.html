About Me

"My mission is the enhance the user experience of everyday products and systems by
incorporating artificial intelligence. I believe that AI has the pontetial to benefit

Hi there!

I'm Youngsu (Tony), a 25-year-old from South Korea currently studying in Australia, with a passion in Artificial Intelligence, and building creative solutions through code. My journey began in 2016 when I watched the iconic Alphago vs Lee Sedol match. Though bittersweet, it sparked my fascination with AI and set me on the path I'm on today. I enrolled in the Bachelor of Computer Science at the University of Queensland in 2019, but due to COVID-19 and mandatory military service, I paused my studies until 2024. During that time, I focused on self-studying machine learning and honing my development skills. I returned to university in 2024 and plan to graduate in 2025. Growing up between Korea and Australia, my love for tech started with video games and evolved into a deep interest in solving real-world problems with code. Today, I'm excited by the possibilities of software and the potential of AI to shape the future.
